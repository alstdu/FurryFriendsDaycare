Our Services

Hover over our services cards to find out more about what's included in each of our wonderful packages! Our services are loved by all. You and your pet will thank us later.

Boarding

Our boarding services provide a safe and clean home away from home for your pets when you are away. We prioritize that your pet stays comfortable with their spaces and receives food, exercise, and companionship from trained staff. We provide you peace of mind, knowing your pets are well-cared while you're away.

What's Included

  • 1-3 Meals
  • Playtime
  • 2-3 long walks
  • Medical Care (if needed)
  • Bath time (if needed)
  • Photo Updates
  • Individual Pet Rooms & Beds
  • Temperature controlled & Secure

Price: $100 per day

Daycare

Our daycare services offer supervised, interactive and fun activities for pets during their stay. They can enjoy playtime, exercise, and socialization with other furry friends. We are committed to ensuring that your pets are well-attended to, receive exercise, and don't experience loneliness or boredom during their stay with us.

What's Included

  • 1 Meal
  • Playtime
  • 1-2 long walks
  • Medical Care (if needed)
  • Bath time (if needed)

Price: $15 per hour

House-sitting

Our house-sitting services involve our trained pet-sitter coming to your home to take care of your beloved fur families. It allows your pets to stay in their familiar environment, reducing stress and anxiety. House-sitting is an excellent choice for pets that are more comfortable in their own space and for owners who want their home to appear occupied while they're away.

What's Included

  • Meals as Instructed by you
  • You set the rules of the house
  • You decide how many walks
  • Medical Care if needed
  • Photo updates of pets
  • Plenty of playtime

Price: Varies by Sitter

Note: please check with sitter about their services

Grooming

As an added bonus to our other services, we also provide drop in grooming services for your pet! Let us know if you'd like to add this service to your daycare or boarding booking, or simply drop in! Please note that our staff will prioritize those with appointments, but we will get your pet in as soon as possible.

What's Included

  • Shampoo & Condition
  • Nail Clipping
  • Brush & Dry
  • Trim (if requested)

Price: $60 per pet
