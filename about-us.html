About Us

History

In 2015, Emily Thompson was looking for pet sitting care for her two small pomeranian princesses. While on her search, she noticed how difficult it was to find a service she could entrust her darlings with. This is all how Furry Friends Daycare came to be. What began as a small, passionate venture has blossomed into a trusted community staple, providing unparalleled care to pets and peace of mind to their owners. Our journey has always been guided by a simple yet profound mission: to bring peace to pet owners, one tail wag at a time. Over the years, our team providing our platform has expanded to include services, features and options to our customers, always while prioritizing the well-being and happiness of the pets in our care.

Services

Furry Friends Daycare delivers a plethora of top-tier, tailored services to cater to your pets' unique needs and ensure they have a delightful experience in your absence. Our offerings include engaging daycare services, secure overnight stays with 24/7 supervision, professional grooming, specialized care for pets with particular needs, dynamic training and enrichment activities, and personalized updates with photos and videos upon request. We're dedicated to providing unparalleled care, ensuring your pets are happy, healthy, and having fun, all while you enjoy peace of mind.
